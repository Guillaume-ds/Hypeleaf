The reason we started this journey:


Sneakers represent 47% of total shoe sales. That's why we wanted to make the most consumed shoe model to have a maximum impact on our scale. We know today that the textile industry alone, at the global level, emits 1.2 billion tons of greenhouse gases, more than the pollution caused by air and sea traffic combined. This represents up to 10% of global carbon emissions.

Moreover, it is a fact that only 1% of clothing and footwear is recycled. This is why we have bet on sustainable and recyclable footwear, so that we can act for the planet and its ecosystem.
